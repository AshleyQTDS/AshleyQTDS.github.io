Biography

Dr. Yuexin Ma is currently a Research Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science, Hong Kong Baptist University. She received her BEng degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University in 2015 and her PhD degree in Computer Science from the University of Hong Kong in 2019. Her research interests include computer vision, perception and planning in robotics and autonomous driving, medical image processing, and computer graphics. Her work has been published in several top journals and conferences, including Science Robotics, AAAI, IJCAI, AAMAS, MICCAI, Siggraph Asia, etc.

My research interests include AI, autonomous driving, robotics, computer vision and computer graphics.
